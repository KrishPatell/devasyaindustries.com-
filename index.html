ABOUT US

Two Decades of Steel Innovation

Devasya Group, established in 2003 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, is a TUV SUD ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company and one of India's leading manufacturers and suppliers of stainless steel products. We specialize in producing a wide range of items including stainless steel wires, bright bars round, hexagonal, square, flat, and custom profiles.

Our 6 state-of-the-art production facilities, including a dedicated Rolling Mill, span 20,000 SQ.MT and have an annual production capacity of approximately 25,000 MT (T). We pride ourselves on our products' high strength, corrosion resistance, thermal stability, dimensional accuracy, and excellent finish.
